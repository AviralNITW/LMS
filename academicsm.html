About the course:

Master of Computer Applications (MCA)

is a postgraduate degree focused on advanced theoretical and practical knowledge in computer applications, software development, and IT management. The course is designed to produce professionals capable of solving complex technical problems in the IT industry. Here's an overview:

  • Duration

    • The MCA course is typically 3 years (6 semesters) for students with a BCA (Bachelor of Computer Applications) B.Sc(Bachelors of science) or related degree.

  • Eligibility:

    • Bachelor's Degree: A graduate in BCA, B.Sc. (Computer Science/IT), or a related field from a recognized university, with minimum of 60% marks.
    • The NIMCET (NIT MCA Common Entrance Test) is an entrance exam for admission into the Master of Computer Applications (MCA) programs offered by the National Institutes of Technology (NITs) in India. Here’s an overview:
